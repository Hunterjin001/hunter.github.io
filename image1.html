The quote "The magic of Christmas never ends. Its greatest gifts are family and friends" conveys a heartfelt message about the enduring spirit of Christmas.
suggests that the true essence of the holiday lies not in material gifts or decorations, but in the relationships and connections we share with loved ones.
The"magic" refers to the joy, warmth, and love that the season brings, which continues beyond the holiday itself.
